Controls:
	Walking:
	WASD - move
	E - enter vehicle, pick up/drop, deposit
	Mouse - move camera
	Scroll wheel - increase/decrease sensitivity
	
	Driving:
	W/S - accelerate/decelerate
	A/D - turn
	Space - Brake
	Mouse - move camera
	Scroll wheel - increase/decrease sensitivity
	E - exit vehicle (only when stopped)
	
	Alt+F4 - exit
